### Profiles

The following profiles have been defined for this implementation guide:

| Profile | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| [GPCCMP CarePlan](StructureDefinition-gpccmp-careplan.html) | A CarePlan profile for documenting a General Practitioner Chronic Condition Management Plan |
| [GPCCMP Goal](StructureDefinition-gpccmp-goal.html) | A Goal profile for documenting health goals within a chronic condition management plan |

### Profile Details

#### GPCCMP CarePlan

The GPCCMP CarePlan profile is designed to capture the essential elements of a chronic condition management plan including:
- Plan status and period
- The chronic conditions being managed
- Treatment goals
- Care activities and interventions
- Care team members

#### GPCCMP Goal

The GPCCMP Goal profile captures health goals established as part of a chronic condition management plan, including:
- Goal description
- Target measures and values
- Due dates
- Related conditions

### Extensions

No custom extensions are currently defined in this implementation guide.
